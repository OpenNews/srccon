Join us

July 24 & 25 at the Chemical Heritage Foundation in Philadelphia’s walkable and historic Old City.

Pitch a session

SRCCON is a highly interactive conference. If you’re planning to attend, we’re accepting session proposals through June 9.

learn about sessions

SRCCON is a conference for developers, interactive designers, and other people who love to code in and near newsrooms. Spend two days collaborating with some of journalism-code’s best thinkers and makers, sketching, planning, and building together.

Join us for two days in peer-to-peer sessions built around conversation and collaboration—no slides, no presentations, no lectures. SRCCON will also have a bonus evening round of short talks and conversations focused on extracurricular interests and individual obsessions, as well as plenty of opportunities for freeform hallway conversations. Registration opens in late May, and tickets will cost $150.

Sessions will be designed with an eye toward continuing work and conversations through SRCCON and after. We expect themes to develop across sessions and are eager to support connections between groups, so please let us know if there’s someone who should be involved in SRCCON who we may not already know.
